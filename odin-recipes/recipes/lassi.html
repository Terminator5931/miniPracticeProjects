Lassi

Description

It is a mouth watering recipe for soothing your dried up throat in summer with cool milk lassi as it uplifts mood and lifts your enery levels up high!

Ingredients

  • Ice Cubes
  • milk
  • yogurt
  • suger
  • Whipping Cream

Steps

  1. Add ingredients in a blender and blend until no longer icy. If you’d like to keep some of the bits of ice, blend for a shorter time.
  2. It’ll naturally become frothy on top.
  3. Taste and add sugar for sweetness, yogurt for more tart flavor, ice to make it cooler.
  4. Pour into two large glasses or 3-4 small. This recipe makes around 4 cups (32 oz), so 2 large lassis or 4 kid-size, though my kids usually demand more than 8 oz.
